# Zensery

## Profile
Zensery (born Luca Zempel) is a German rapper from Berlin, known for his distinctive style and regional Berlin dialect in his music. He emerged in the German rap scene around 2016 and has gained popularity with hits like "Puffpuppe" and "La Casa De Papel".

## Background
- Real name: Luca Zempel
- Born in Berlin, Germany
- Currently resides in Brandenburg
- Started music career in 2016
- Stage name given by a friend

## Music Career
- Debuted with regional Berlin rap style
- Known for authentic street narratives and humor
- Gained mainstream attention with viral songs
- Part of the German trap and cloud rap scene

## Discography

- Various singles and EPs
- Collaborations with other German artists
