# KuchenTV

## Profile
KuchenTV (real name: André) is a popular German YouTuber and streamer known for his comedy content, gaming videos, and reaction formats. He has built a large following through humorous commentary on internet trends, gaming, and everyday life. KuchenTV is part of the German YouTube scene and has expanded into streaming and merchandise.
