# Hanybal

## Profile
Hanybal (born October 22, 1983) is a German rapper from Frankfurt am Main, known for his work in the German gangsta rap scene. He gained recognition through collaborations and his distinctive style, though he remains more of an underground figure compared to mainstream artists.

## Background
- Born in Frankfurt, Germany to a German mother and Egyptian father
- Former amateur footballer in Frankfurt-Heddernheim
- Started rap career in 2006
- Co-founded rap duo "439" with rapper Solo
- Signed to Azad's label Bozz Music
- Known for gangsta rap themes and street narratives
