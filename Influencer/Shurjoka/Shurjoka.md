# Shurjoka

## Profile
Shurjoka (real name: Shurik) is a German rapper and social media personality known for his music in the German hip-hop scene. He has gained attention through his unique style, collaborations, and online presence. Shurjoka combines humor, street elements, and catchy beats in his work.
