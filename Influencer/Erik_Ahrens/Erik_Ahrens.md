# Erik Ahrens

## Profile
Erik Ahrens is a German social media influencer and strategist known for his work in promoting far-right politics, particularly for the AfD (Alternative for Germany). He serves as a key figure in the AfD's TikTok strategy, helping the party gain visibility among young voters. Ahrens has been involved in controversial activities, including leaked videos showing extremist views and connections to neo-Nazi networks.

## Background
- Born and raised in Germany
- Involved in right-wing activism since youth
- Former member of the Junge Alternative (AfD youth organization)
- Known for patriotic and nationalist rhetoric
- Has faced conflicts and exited from some right-wing scenes

## Social Media and Platforms
- **TikTok**: Primary platform for AfD content and strategy
- **Twitter/X**: Political commentary and networking

## Key Activities
- TikTok strategist for AfD campaigns
- Organizer of right-wing events and meetings

- Connections to international far-right networks

## Controversies
- Leaked videos showing "Führer fantasies" and extremist rhetoric
- Accusations of neo-Nazi sympathies
- Involvement in antisemitic incidents
- Conflicts with other right-wing figures
