# Naomi Seibt

## Profile
Naomi Seibt is a German YouTube influencer and activist, often described as the "anti-Greta" in opposition to climate activist Greta Thunberg. She is known for her conservative views, support for the AfD (Alternative for Germany), and skepticism towards climate change activism.
