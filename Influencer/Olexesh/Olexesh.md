# Olexesh

## Profile
Olexesh (born February 25, 1988) is a German rapper from Darmstadt, known for his breakthrough success in the German rap charts. He became the first rapper to reach number one on the German album charts with his album "Rolexesh" in 2018. Olexesh is recognized for his street rap style and Kranichstein district roots.

## Background
- Born Olexij Kosarev in Darmstadt, Germany
- Grew up in the Kranichstein district
- Started music career in street rap scene
- Known for authentic street narratives
- Breakthrough with mainstream success
