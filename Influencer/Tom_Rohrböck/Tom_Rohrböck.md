# Tom Rohrböck

## Profile
Tom Rohrböck is a German political influencer and strategist associated with right-wing politics, particularly the AfD (Alternative for Germany). He has been described as a "right-wing phantom" due to his behind-the-scenes influence and connections. Rohrböck has been involved in various political campaigns, media ventures, and has faced scrutiny for his role in right-wing networks.
